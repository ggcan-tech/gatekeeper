[run 3]
- **no complaint-specific deficiency shown** — It exploits that the motion never ties its Rule 12(b)(6) conclusions to the actual allegations of this Complaint, does not show that one or more required elements are absent, identifies no allegation on the face of the Complaint that defeats satisfaction or excuse of any condition precedent, and recites Rule 9(b) at a high level without actually engaging the pleaded allegations defendant by defendant and statement by statement. [run 4]
- **standards-first complaint-specific failure** — It exploits that Defendants’ motion fails at the threshold by never engaging the governing Rule 12(b)(6) plausibility standard in a complaint-specific way and instead demanding more detail, more proof, and a quotation of contract language. [run 5]

## What we'd fix before filing

- Rewrite the motion to walk each challenged claim element by element against the Complaint’s actual allegations, quoting or pinpointing the pleaded facts and explaining why they fail under Rule 8, Twombly, and Iqbal rather than labeling them “conclusory” in the abstract. [run 1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
- Reframe the contract argument to confront Rule 9(c) directly, identify authority supporting any requirement to plead a precise contractual provision at the Rule 12 stage, and explain why the pleaded contractual duty, breach, performance or excuse, and damages still fail to give fair notice. [run 1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
- Tighten the fraud argument by addressing the complaint’s pleaded statements, speakers, timing, place, content, and attribution as to each defendant and showing specifically what Rule 9(b) still leaves missing, instead of reciting the “who, what, when, where, how” formula. [run 1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
- If seeking dismissal with prejudice, add a developed amendment-history and futility section showing what defects were previously identified to plaintiff and why the remaining defects are substantive rather than curable by a more definite pleading. [run 2, 5]
